Tell me what to change

Review, right on the slide R

  • See the round pencil button at the top-right? Click it — or press R — to open the review panel.
  • Type what you'd like changed on this slide and hit Send to agent (or Ctrl+Enter).
  • A green ● watcher live badge means I'm listening — your comment reaches me the moment you send it.
  • Want a comment about one specific thing? Hover it and click 📌 Attach — I'll change exactly that element.
  • Try it now: open the panel and leave a comment on this slide.

Fix small things yourself

Edit text inline E

  • Press E (or the Edit mode button) to make the text editable.
  • Click any line, fix the typo, then click away — small fixes save straight to the file.
  • Anything bigger? Leave it as a comment instead, and I'll handle it.
  • Press Esc to leave edit mode or close the panel.

When it's ready
